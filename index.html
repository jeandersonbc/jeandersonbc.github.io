About me

I am currently an undergraduate student (senior) in Computer Science at Federal University of Campina Grande (UFCG), Brazil. During my undergraduate studies, I was a visiting student at Walsh University (North Canton - Ohio, USA, Fall 2012 - Spring 2013) and Research Assistant at University of Massachusetts (Amherst - Massachusetts, USA - Summer 2013). Since my freshman year (second semester), I have been involved in researches related to Software Engineer. Today, I am associated with the Software Productivity Group. I am studying how to make refactorings safer and how to make automated refactoring tools more practical to developers. When I am not studying, I like to spend some time solving programming problems and taking Computer Science classes online.
